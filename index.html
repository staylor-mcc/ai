Teaching & Management Philosophy

As a dedicated Management Instructor at McLennan Community College, my ultimate objective is to cultivate the organizational, entrepreneurial, and leadership competencies that drive dynamic enterprises.

By linking structural management frameworks to contemporary corporate scenarios, I ensure my students step into the business landscape equipped to organize, delegate, strategy-map, and lead team members with clear intent. Every student carries the potential to make a stellar organizational impact.

BMGT 1327: Principles of Management

A foundational exploration of management roles, organizational design, motivational theory, decision-making dynamics, and systemic team supervision. This course builds key competencies for effective contemporary leaders.
